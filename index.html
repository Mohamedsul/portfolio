01 — Retail / E-commerce
Customer Retention Intelligence
Predictive Churn · CLV Modeling
35%
Reduction in churn rate within 6 months
Business Problem

A mid-market retailer was losing 18% of customers annually with no early-warning system. Retention spend was reactive and poorly targeted, burning margin without improving outcomes.

Decision Gap

Commercial teams had no visibility into at-risk accounts until cancellation. Intervention windows were being missed by 4–6 weeks, after the decision to leave was already made.

AI Solution

Engineered a churn prediction model (XGBoost + behavioral features) with 92% accuracy at 30-day horizon. Integrated outputs into CRM for automated segment-specific interventions.

Trade-off Navigated

Chose interpretable models over higher-accuracy black boxes—enabling the commercial team to trust, interrogate, and act on scores without data science mediation.

Key Insight: The biggest lever wasn't model accuracy—it was reducing the lag between signal and action. Connecting predictions directly to CRM workflows generated 3× the impact of the model improvement alone.

02 — Financial Services
Real-Time Fraud Prevention System
Anomaly Detection · Risk Intelligence
$2M+
In projected annual fraud losses prevented
Business Problem

A fintech platform experienced rising fraudulent transaction volume that existing rule-based filters failed to catch. False positives were also blocking 4% of legitimate high-value transactions.

Decision Gap

The risk team was making manual review decisions on 600+ flagged transactions per day—unsustainable volume with no intelligence layer to prioritize where human judgment was actually needed.

AI Solution

Deployed a real-time anomaly detection layer (Isolation Forest + network graph features) via Kafka, achieving 94% precision with sub-50ms inference. Reduced manual review queue by 70%.

Trade-off Navigated

Accepted a slightly lower recall rate to minimize false positives—the client's unit economics made blocking a legitimate transaction more costly than missing a low-value fraud event.

Key Insight: Fraud models should be calibrated to business unit economics, not just statistical performance. Optimizing for the client's specific cost asymmetry unlocked 40% more value than a generic precision-recall balance would have.

03 — Enterprise SaaS
Real-Time Commercial Intelligence
BI Architecture · Decision Automation
60%
Faster executive decision cycles
Business Problem

A SaaS company's commercial leadership operated on monthly reporting cycles. By the time insights reached the table, the operating window to act on them had already closed.

Decision Gap

Leaders were flying blind intra-month. Pipeline health, product usage signals, and expansion triggers existed in siloed systems—no unified real-time view of commercial performance.

AI Solution

Architected a unified data pipeline (1M+ events/day via Azure + SQL Server) feeding a Power BI intelligence layer with automated anomaly alerts, churn signals, and expansion opportunity scoring.

Trade-off Navigated

Prioritized a curated set of 12 high-signal metrics over comprehensive reporting—reducing cognitive load and increasing the probability that leaders would act on what they saw.

Key Insight: The most dangerous dashboard is one with too many metrics. Ruthless prioritization of what leaders actually need to decide—not everything they could possibly see—was the design choice that drove adoption.

04 — Logistics / Operations
Automated Data Infrastructure
Data Engineering · ETL Architecture
80%
Reduction in manual data processing time
Business Problem

A logistics company's analytics team spent 60% of its bandwidth on data wrangling—manually reconciling feeds from 7 source systems before any analysis could begin.

Decision Gap

Insights arrived 5–7 days after operational events—too late to influence the decisions that generated them. The analytics function was a historian, not a decision partner.

AI Solution

Designed and deployed a cloud-native ETL pipeline on AWS (Lambda + S3 + Airflow) with automated data quality validation, reducing time-to-insight from 7 days to under 4 hours.

Trade-off Navigated

Chose a modular, source-agnostic architecture over a tightly integrated solution—higher upfront investment, but enabling the client to onboard two additional data sources post-engagement without external support.

Key Insight: Data infrastructure is a strategic asset, not a technical cost center. The engagement ROI compounded over 18 months as the client's team used the freed capacity to build five new analytical products independently.
