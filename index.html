01 — Retail / E-commerce
Customer Retention Intelligence
Predictive Churn · CLV Modeling
35%
Churn rate reduced
Business Problem

A mid-market retailer was losing 18% of customers annually with no early-warning system. Retention spend was reactive, burning margin without improving outcomes.

Decision Gap

Commercial teams had no visibility into at-risk accounts until cancellation — intervention windows missed by 4–6 weeks, after the decision to leave was already made.

AI Solution

Churn prediction model (XGBoost + behavioral features) with 92% accuracy at 30-day horizon. Outputs integrated into CRM for automated, segment-specific interventions.

Trade-off Navigated

Chose interpretable models over higher-accuracy black boxes — enabling the commercial team to act on scores without data science mediation every time.

Key Insight: The biggest lever wasn't model accuracy — it was reducing lag between signal and action. Connecting predictions to CRM workflows generated 3× the impact of any model improvement alone.

02 — Financial Services
Real-Time Fraud Prevention
Anomaly Detection · Risk Intelligence
$2M+
Fraud losses prevented
Business Problem

A fintech platform faced rising fraud that existing rule-based filters failed to catch. False positives were also blocking 4% of legitimate high-value transactions.

Decision Gap

The risk team made manual review decisions on 600+ flagged transactions daily — unsustainable with no intelligence layer to prioritize where human judgment was actually needed.

AI Solution

Real-time anomaly detection (Isolation Forest + network graph features) via Kafka, achieving 94% precision at sub-50ms inference. Manual review queue reduced by 70%.

Trade-off Navigated

Accepted slightly lower recall to minimize false positives — the client's unit economics made blocking a legitimate transaction more costly than missing a low-value fraud event.

Key Insight: Fraud models should be calibrated to business economics, not just statistical performance. Optimizing for the client's cost asymmetry unlocked 40% more value than a generic precision-recall balance would have.

03 — Enterprise SaaS
Real-Time Commercial Intelligence
BI Architecture · Decision Automation
60%
Faster decision cycles
Business Problem

A SaaS company's commercial leadership operated on monthly reporting cycles. By the time insights arrived, the window to act on them had already closed.

Decision Gap

Leaders were flying blind intra-month. Pipeline health, product usage signals, and expansion triggers existed in siloed systems — no unified real-time view.

AI Solution

Unified data pipeline (1M+ events/day) feeding a Power BI intelligence layer with automated anomaly alerts, churn signals, and expansion opportunity scoring.

Trade-off Navigated

Prioritized 12 high-signal metrics over comprehensive reporting — reducing cognitive load and increasing the probability that leaders would actually act on what they saw.

Key Insight: The most dangerous dashboard has too many metrics. Ruthless prioritization of what leaders need to decide — not everything they could see — was the choice that drove real adoption.

04 — Logistics / Operations
Automated Data Infrastructure
Data Engineering · ETL Architecture
80%
Less manual processing
Business Problem

A logistics company's analytics team spent 60% of its bandwidth on data wrangling — manually reconciling 7 source systems before any analysis could begin.

Decision Gap

Insights arrived 5–7 days after operational events — too late to influence the decisions that generated them. The analytics function was a historian, not a decision partner.

AI Solution

Cloud-native ETL pipeline on AWS (Lambda + S3 + Airflow) with automated data quality validation, reducing time-to-insight from 7 days to under 4 hours.

Trade-off Navigated

Chose a modular, source-agnostic architecture over a tightly integrated solution — enabling the client to onboard two additional data sources post-engagement independently.

Key Insight: Data infrastructure is a strategic asset. The engagement ROI compounded over 18 months as freed analyst capacity funded five new analytical products built entirely in-house.
